== Description ==

# SitePal for WordPress Plugin

Speaking characters are a proven way to increase traffic and readership of your blog by keeping your blog unique and fresh. A SitePal speaking character is a great way to personalize your blog, keep your content updated, and connect with your readers. Using SitePal's powerful, self-service editing tool, it's a breeze to design a character to your liking, add your audio to it, and publish it live on your blog.

1. Personalize Your Blog with Your Very Own Digital Character Presence and Your own Voice
2. Provide Side Commentary to your Posts 
3. Elevate Important Points and Announcements with a Spoken Call to Action to Key Areas of Your Blog 
4. Automated Photo Upload and Conversion into a Virtual Speaking Character


== Installation ==
 
1. Upload the `sitepal-talking-avatar` folder containing all the files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress. The plugin is called 'Sitepal Talking Avatar 1.5'.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What if I don't have account? =

Go to the SitePal plugin options page and click the "Get It" button. Follow the instructions to signup for a 15 day free trial.

= How do I configure my SitePal account for Wordpress? =

If you already have an account, go to the SitePal options page. Enter your username and password and click the save. Select your Account from the Account list if you have multiple accounts with the same credentials.

= How do I embed using the Plugin? =

Start by selecting a post or page to edit. Notice the integrated SitePal embed wizard. Place the cursor in the desired location where you would like you scene to live on your page or post. Select from the Scene you created in your SitePal account, set your potions and once ready click the "Add" To Post?button.
