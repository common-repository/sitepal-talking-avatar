Input your Log in credentials
Enter your account email address and your password, and then click SAVE.
Note: If you forgot your account password click the “forgot your password” link

Selecting your account
Select your account from the list of accounts, if you only have one account it will be selected automatically. Click the SAVE button when your selection is complete.

Note: Your username, password and account are saved in the WordPress database and only need to set once.
